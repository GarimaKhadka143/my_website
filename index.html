About SKYROOT GARDEN

SKYROOT GARDENS is your Urban Gardening Partner in Nepal. If you've alwayes wanted a garden but lack of the space , terrace gardening could be the answer.Take advantage of this urban gardening technique thats provides you a fresh produce,flowers or vegetables.

Key Benefits of Urban Terrace Gardening:-

- Space Optimization: Utilizes rooftops and terraces for gardening, making use of otherwise unused areas.

- Environmental Impact: Helps reduce urban heat, improves air quality, and supports biodiversity.

- Self-Sufficiency: Enables individuals to grow their own organic produce, reducing dependency on store-bought vegetables.

- Aesthetic Appeal: Enhances the beauty of urban homes with lush greenery and vibrant flowers.

How to Start a Skyroot Garden:-

- Choose the Right Plants: Opt for vegetables, herbs, and flowers that thrive in rooftop conditions.

- Use Lightweight Containers: Ensure pots and planters are suitable for rooftop gardening.

- Implement Proper Drainage: Prevent waterlogging by using well-draining soil and containers.

- Consider Vertical Gardening: Utilize walls and hanging planters to maximize space.

- Sustainable Practices: Use compost, rainwater harvesting, and organic fertilizers for eco-friendly gardening.
